Dr. MR Doreswamy Silver Jubilee Block

A Block

The heart of the college and named after our own pro-chancellor, MR Doreswamy. It's the first block any PESU student usually visits, for admission procedure. But that's not it. Apart from the administrative department, this block has a huge Auditorium cum basketball court, a library, the COE Office, the classes of the MCA department and a few Biotech labs. Don't miss the Vedic tea sold outside the auditorium gates.
