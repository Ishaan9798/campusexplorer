B Block

The main engineering block where you find most of the engineering population. The most recently made, this block is comprised of 14 floors each of which is department specific. All the floors from the ground to the second floor is alocated to the CS Department. Third floor is for Architechture and Design. Fourth and Fifth is for ECE department. Next three are for Mechanical Dept. The rest of the floors are still under construction. Every classroom has it's own Tv installed. Apart from these classrooms, this block also has a huge number of labs. Outside this block, you'll find the infamous OAT.
